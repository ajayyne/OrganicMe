A Little About Us...

History

The Organic Me farm has been a pioneer for organic farming in the Scotland. The lush fields of the farm were initially cultivated to grow a variety of nutritious fruits and vegetables and over the years, we have expanded to producing our own honey from a thriving apiary and maintaining beehives nestled amongst the vibrant flora. Today, Organic Me stands as a testament to generations of dedication towards promoting health, sustainability, and an intimate connection with nature.

Things To Do Around The Area

STICHILL LINN

Discover this 2.6-km out-and-back trail near Kelso, Scottish Borders. Generally considered an easy route, it takes an average of 38 min to complete. This trail is great for birding and walking.

BEIRHOPE ALPACAS

Beirhope offers a unique Alpaca trecking experience. From the Cheviot Hills to off-grid, eco friendly camping in the most beautiful South of Scotland location. Get closer to nature and immerse youself in the sights and sounds of the countryside.

FLOORS CASTLE

Explore the beautiful castle and grounds of Floors Castle. Victorian Walled Gardens with glasshouses, herbaceous borders and a formal Millennium Garden. Suitable for the whole family, Floors has so much to discover.

WHITE LAW CURR

Get to know this 11.1-km circular trail near Kelso. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 3 h 50 min to complete. This trail is great for birding, hiking, and walking.

We love The Borders! The Scottish Borders is rich in beautiful countryside and farmland. We are grateful to run our community centered farm right here in Kelso. You can find lots of great walks, good food and friendly faces in the town.

As the seasons change, so does our farm. Every season we like to spice things up, no two visits to the farm will be the same. In the summer we love to keep our doors open later, and in winter we like to slow it down a little bit and embrace rest and re-building. Some of the areas of the farm may be closed during the winter months, please always refer to our opening times before planning a trip, or contact us directly.

Throughout the year we host a number of events for the whole family to enjoy. Everything from spring easter egg hunts to pumpkin patch fun in the fall. Sign up to our newsletter at the Farm Shop or Cafe to hear more about all the exciting things we have to offer, delivered straight to your door.

Kind Earth Strategy 2025

The "Kind Earth Strategy" is a visionary approach that seeks to achieve a set of sustainable goals by promoting harmony and cooperation between mankind and the environment. This strategy emphasizes regenerative practices, such as reforestation initiatives, renewable energy adoption, and responsible waste management, to foster biodiversity conservation, mitigate climate change, and ensure equitable resource distribution for a better and more sustainable future. By 2025 we aim to fully implement our strategy so that we can keep working towards a cleaner and greener future for ourselves and the Earth.
