About Us

Organic Me was founded in 2018. Based in Kelso, Scottish Borders, we are passionate about providing the best and brightest produce on our 10 Acres of farmland. We are especially proud to launch our very own Organic Me Honey. Visit us to explore our range of organic Homeware, Clothing, and Wellness products.

OUR FARM

We own 10 Acres of gorgeous land in the Scottish Borders where we guarantee you’ll find the best and brightest fresh produce. Come and meet our cows, sheep, goats, and chickens. Bring the family for a great day out in Kelso.

ORGANIC CAFE

The Roost is our on site cafe dedicated to creating delicious organic sweet treats and serving sustainable coffee. Our Beans are fair trade and our food is always locally sourced. Pop in for a bite to eat after exploring our farm!

OUR MISSION

Organic Me aims to deliver the best local organic food and products in Scotland. We're proud of the hard work that goes into producing our honey, sourcing our products, and running our farm. By 2025 we aim to implement 'Kind Earth'.

Based In The Borders

Visit and explore the picturesque landscape of The Borders. Our organic farm is a hub for sustainable agriculture. With a strong commitment to natural farming methods, we cultivate a variety of crops and raise livestock without the use of artificial chemicals or GMO's. Our farm also serves as an educational center, welcoming visitors of all ages to learn about the benefits of organic farming and how to reconnect with nature.

Farm Shop

The shop is a haven for conscious consumers seeking organic and sustainable products. You can find clothing made from environmentally friendly fabrics (such as organic cotton and hemp), homeware made from recycled materials and the top wellness products in Scotland, all from local suppliers. We aim to offer a holistic shopping experience, connecting customers with nature and providing an array of consciously sourced products that support a healthier and more environmentally friendly lifestyle.

The Roost

After all of your exploring, take a rest in The Roost Cafe.
With its commitment to sustainability and the use of local ingredients, we serve up nature itself. As you step inside, the earthy scent of freshly brewed fair-trade coffee fills the air, inviting you to indulge in a flavorful cup sourced from local suppliers. From colorful salads with farm-fresh vegetables to delicious brownies and cakes. Enjoy a bite at The Roost!
