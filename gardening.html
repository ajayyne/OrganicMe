Gardening Courses

We believe that gardening is not merely a hobby, but a lifelong passion that can bring joy, peace and a deeper connection to nature. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced gardener looking to expand your skills, we offer a variety of courses to meet your needs and interests.

FLOWERS & PLANTS

Learn to grow a colourful gardening starting with in season flowers and decorative plants

3 WEEKS £15

SOIL & COMPOST

Explore the different types of organic and natural soil and compost, so your produce can grow big and bright

3 WEEKS £20

SAPPLINGS & SEEDS

Dive into germination techniques and sowing seeds and saplings. A great course for beginners.

2 WEEKS £15

LITTLE SPROUTS

A beginners course for children. Help your little one plant, water, and watch their seeds grow

4 WEEKS £30

GARDEN VEG

Juicy Tomatoes, Bright Carrots, Leafy Lettuce and Snap Peas. All of these you can learn to grow here on the farm

6 WEEKS £50

ALLOTMENT HIRE

We’re not greedy! Hire an allotment for personal use to grow whatever fruit, veg, or plants you’d like

FROM £10 A MONTH
